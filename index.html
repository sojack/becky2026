The Athlete

Becky Yuan is a long-distance open water swimmer with two serious attempts behind her — her first English Channel crossing in 2024 and a Catalina Channel attempt in 2025, both cut short by violent weather, not by fitness. This August 18, she enters the water again: 33 km from England to France.

A multi-sport endurance athlete since 2016, she placed 3rd in her age category at the Canadaman X-Tri — Canada's toughest full-distance triathlon — and made history as the first Chinese Canadian to swim across Lake Erie (20 km, 2022). In 2023 she completed a 42 km relay crossing of Lake Ontario. Every dollar raised goes to The Alexa Trust, supporting families with babies in neonatal intensive care.

The Cause

The Alexa Trust is a UK-registered charity providing vital practical and emotional support to parents with babies in neonatal intensive care. It was founded in memory of Alexa James, born prematurely in 2015.

The Alexa Trust is one of the most frequently chosen charities for English Channel crossings worldwide — your brand joins a community of supporters with global reach in the open water swimming world.
